Core ideas & conclusions

Bitcoin Fundamentals & Conviction

  • Bitcoin is the only truly risk-free asset long-term; all else carries hidden risks.
  • “Not your keys, not your coins” — self-custody is non-negotiable.
  • The money printer is a permanent feature of fiat; Bitcoin deletes it.
  • Most people will never beat Bitcoin’s CAGR — unless you have a true edge, just hold.

Debt Strategy & Leverage

  • Fiat debt is a short-dollar position; in Bitcoin terms, it’s often an appreciating asset.
  • Properly timed debt cycles can massively accelerate stack growth with minimal risk.
  • Limitations are mostly credit underwriting, not economic fundamentals.
  • The real constraint is total credit extended to your SSN/entity, not “affordability” per se.

Wealth as % of the Pie

  • In a fixed-supply system, your stack size = your lifetime wealth ceiling.
  • Consumption erodes your share; productivity increases it.
  • Once fully in BTC, you must create/capture value or your share shrinks relative to others.

Competitive Reality in a Hyperbitcoinized World

  • Bitcoin + Starlink + AI = global equalization of opportunity.
  • Your real competition is planet-wide, including ultra-hungry, low-cost, high-output talent.
  • The head start window is closing; now is the time for asymmetric action.

Preparation & Morality

  • Hyperbitcoinization will erase safety nets; merit-based survival will be brutal.
  • Build capital buffers, skills, and networks before the fiat system collapses.
  • Compassion vs. utility will be a defining moral question for Bitcoiners.
  • Expect political backlash, scapegoating, and regulatory attacks — be sovereign.

Life Alignment

  • Align all major decisions to the Bitcoin trajectory (career, residence, citizenship).
  • Avoid fiat entanglements that can be used against you.
  • Model all major moves in Excel; intuition is no substitute for math.

Practical Warnings

  • ETFs, custodial platforms, and paper BTC carry systemic and counterparty risk.
  • Legal and political systems will adapt slowly — don’t assume fairness or consistency.
  • Leverage, if mis-timed, can destroy you; discipline > conviction.
